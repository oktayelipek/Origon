# Progress Stepper

Numbered progress dots + connecting lines. Ideal for onboarding wizards,
multi-step forms, KYC flows. Source: Figma `- Progress Stepper` (`12:54443`).

## Accessibility

- Rendered as `<ol>` — screen readers announce ordered sequence.
- The active step carries `aria-current="step"`.
- Completed steps show a checkmark instead of the number.
