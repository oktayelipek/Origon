# Ticker

Infinite horizontal marquee. Ideal for price feeds and announcement banners.
Duplicates content once for a seamless loop. Source: Figma `- Ticker` (`12:3551`).

Rendered with `role="marquee"` — screen readers may skip the animation.
Announcements should also live in an aria-live region elsewhere.
