# Drawer

Overlay panel — bottom sheet on mobile, right rail on desktop. Source: Figma
`Drawer` (page `12:86222`). Portals into `document.body`, adds a semi-opaque
backdrop, and traps `Escape` to close.

## Behavior

- Body scroll is locked while open.
- Escape closes.
- Backdrop click closes.
- Portal target is `document.body` — z-index 999 (backdrop), 1000 (panel).
- Animations: 220ms cubic-bezier slide, 160ms fade for backdrop.
